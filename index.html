Short Bio

Qi WANG received the B.Eng. degree in information security from Unversity of Science and Technology (USTC) in 2007, and the Ph.D. degree in computer science and engineering from The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST) in 2011. Before joining RIKEN as a research scientist, he was a senior software engineer at Baidu and then an assistant professor at the University of Tokyo. is currently an Associate Professor at Department of Computer Science and Engieering (CSE), Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech).
